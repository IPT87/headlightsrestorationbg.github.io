Service
Over time, headlights can become yellowed, faded, and cloudy, reducing the amount of light they project onto the road. This makes night driving more difficult and can affect your safety. Headlight restoration service brings your headlights back to life removing oxidation and restoring their original shine, clarity, and brightness, helping you see the road more clearly at night.
Headlight Restoration Process
  • Thorough cleaning to remove built-up dirt and surface contaminants.
  • Removal of the faded, yellowed oxidized layer on the headlight lens.
  • Multi-stage polishing process to restore clarity, shine, and smooth finish.
  • Application of a ceramic UV protective coating with up to 2 years warranty*.
